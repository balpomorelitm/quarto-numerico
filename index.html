QUARTO

How to Play Quarto

Objective: Create a line of 4 pieces that share at least one common characteristic.

Pieces have 4 characteristics:

  • Height: Tall or Short
  • Color: Dark or Light
  • Shape: Square or Round
  • Fill: Solid or Hollow

How to play:

  1. Player 1 selects a piece for Player 2 by saying its number in Spanish
  2. Player 2 places that piece on the board by saying the cell number in Spanish
  3. Player 2 then selects a piece for Player 1
  4. Continue until someone creates a line of 4 pieces sharing a characteristic

Win conditions: 4 in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) with a common trait.
